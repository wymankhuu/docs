*What shipped in V2 and what's coming next.*

V2 of Playlab shipped to new organizations as the default in May 2026. Existing orgs migrate on a rolling schedule with their Learning Partners through July. This entry summarizes what changed and links to the deeper guides for each area.

## Highlights

Five capabilities define the V2 release.

**Editor model for app collaboration.** Sharing an app with a peer no longer requires adding them to the workspace. Use the Share modal on any app and pick Editor or Viewer. See [Collaborating on an app](https://learn.playlab.ai/getstarted/Collaborating%20on%20an%20App).

**Sharing with individuals, groups, and orgs.** A single Share modal handles every sharing destination. Activity stays segmented by recipient. See [Sharing with individuals](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/Sharing%20with%20Individuals) and [Sharing with groups and orgs](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/Sharing%20with%20Groups%20and%20Orgs).

**Collections.** A curated bundle of apps you can share like a single resource. Updates flow to recipients automatically. The model that ONE8 Foundation, district admins, and curriculum partners asked for. See [What are Collections](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/collections/What%20are%20Collections).

**Centralized org monitoring and flags.** Org admins see usage, member detail, and flagged messages across every workspace from one dashboard. See [Centralized monitoring](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/Reviewing%20App%20Activity).

**Privacy and granular building permissions.** Apps start private to the owner and org admins. Workspaces have three toggles for what members can do. See [App privacy](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/App%20Privacy%20and%20Visibility) and [Workspace building permissions](https://learn.playlab.ai/getstarted/Workspace%20Building%20Permissions).

## What changed for each persona

**Builders.** Privacy by default, the new Editor model, deployment without remixing. See [V2 for Builders](https://learn.playlab.ai/whatsnew/v2/V2%20for%20Builders).

**Educators.** Curating apps from across the org, member detail visibility, granular building permissions per workspace. See [V2 for Educators](https://learn.playlab.ai/whatsnew/v2/V2%20for%20Educators).

**Admins.** Org-wide dashboards, centralized flagged messages, and a clear answer to "what has this student been doing." See [V2 for Admins](https://learn.playlab.ai/whatsnew/v2/V2%20for%20Admins).

**Networks or Districts.** Multi-org deployment, segmented activity, Collections for curated partner bundles. See [V2 for Networks or Districts](https://learn.playlab.ai/whatsnew/v2/V2%20for%20Networks%20or%20Districts).

## Coming next

A few things shipped on a follow-up timeline. They are tracked openly:

App-level insights dashboard for app builders (in development)

Org-level cross-workspace insights aggregation (June 12, 2026)

Rostering support for V2 (in scoping)

For the full list, see [Known limitations](https://learn.playlab.ai/whatsnew/v2/Known%20Limitations).
