## <Icon icon="lightbulb" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> What to Expect

Each Skills Boost session includes:

- **Practical Examples**: Real-world applications and use cases
- **Hands-on Practice**: Interactive exercises and implementation tips
- **Q&A Time**: Direct access to Playlab experts for your specific questions
- **Resource Sharing**: Access to templates, guides, and additional materials
- **Community Connection**: Network with other Playlab members and educators

Each App Showcase session includes:

- **Live Demonstrations**: See apps in action with real-world examples
- **Creator Insights**: Learn directly from the app builders about their design process
- **Remix Opportunities**: Explore how you can adapt these apps for your own context
- **Implementation Tips**: Practical advice for building similar applications
- **Community Q&A**: Interactive discussion with creators and participants
