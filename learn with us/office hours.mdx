## Need Help?
Looking for additional support with Playlab? Whether you are looking for a thought partner or somebody to help you improve your Playlab app? We're here to support you every step of the way with our team of Learning Engineers and coaches.

Schedule time with our team to get personalized support and guidance for your Playlab implementation. Our experts are available to help with questions, troubleshooting, and best practices.

## When to use Office Hours

A few common reasons partners book a session:

- **Troubleshooting an app** that's not behaving the way you expected. Bring the app and a sample conversation.
- **Thought-partnering on a new app design.** We help shape the prompt, references, and scope so the first build lands.
- **V2 migration questions.** Walk through the changes for your org. The [migration guide](/whatsnew/v2/Migrating%20from%20V1%20to%20V2) is the written companion.

<Note>
  If your org has a Learning Partner, reach out to them first — they have direct context on your deployment. Office Hours and `support@playlab.ai` are for general questions and self-serve users.
</Note>
