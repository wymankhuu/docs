## Which live learning option fits?

Playlab runs several live learning formats. This page covers the open onboarding workshop. Other options:

- **V2 Power Up** — partner-LP-led migration training, scheduled directly with your Learning Partner. For partner orgs moving from V1 to V2.
- **Weekly workshops** (Skills Boost + App Showcases) — Wednesdays at 4PM and 7PM ET. Deeper dives on features and community-built apps. See [Playlab weekly workshops](/learn%20with%20us/workshops).
- **PLCs** — cohort-based, semester-long professional learning communities for 101 and 201 levels. See [Fall PLCs](/learn%20with%20us/plc).

**What You'll Learn:**
- **Generative AI Fundamentals** - Understanding the technology behind modern AI tools
- **Playlab Platform Deep Dive** - Hands-on exploration of features and capabilities
- **Building Your First AI App** - Step-by-step guidance through the creation process
- **Community Connection** - Introduction to our supportive educator network

**Why Choose Live Onboarding?**
- **Real-time Problem Solving** - Get instant answers to your specific questions
- **Peer Learning** - Connect with other educators in your session
- **Hands-on Practice** - Build actual AI applications during the session
- **Ongoing Support** - Access to follow-up resources and community
