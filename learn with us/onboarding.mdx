## What you'll learn

- **Generative AI fundamentals** — the technology behind modern AI tools
- **Playlab platform deep dive** — hands-on exploration of features and capabilities
- **Building your first AI app** — step-by-step guidance through the creation process
- **Community connection** — introduction to our educator network

## Which live learning option fits?

Playlab runs several live learning formats. This page covers the open onboarding workshop. Other options:

- **V2 Power Up** — partner-LP-led migration training, scheduled directly with your Learning Partner. For partner orgs moving from V1 to V2.
- **Office Hours** — drop-in support sessions for troubleshooting, app design help, or migration questions. See [Live office hours](/learn%20with%20us/office%20hours).
