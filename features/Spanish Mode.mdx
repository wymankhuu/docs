## <Icon icon="bell-on" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> What is this feature?

Spanish Mode allows you to configure your Playlab applications to interact with users in Spanish. This feature enables natural language processing, responses, and user interactions entirely in Spanish, making your apps accessible to Spanish-speaking audiences.

<Warning>
Spanish Mode changes the primary language of your app's interactions. Ensure your prompts and content are appropriately configured for Spanish-speaking users.
</Warning>

## <Icon icon="lightbulb" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Rationale for the feature

Reaching Spanish-speaking audiences is essential for global applications. Spanish Mode ensures that your Playlab app can communicate naturally and effectively with users in their preferred language, improving accessibility and user experience.

## <Icon icon="star" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Tips for Using Spanish Mode Effectively

### <Icon icon="target" size="20" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use culturally appropriate content">
    Ensure your prompts and responses consider cultural nuances and regional variations in Spanish-speaking markets.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Test with native speakers">
    Have native Spanish speakers review your app to identify any awkward phrasing or translation issues that automated systems might miss.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Consider regional variations">
    Spanish varies by region (Spain, Mexico, Argentina, etc.). Consider which variant is most appropriate for your target audience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Maintain consistency in terminology">
    Use consistent Spanish terminology throughout your app to avoid confusing users with different words for the same concept.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## <Icon icon="circle-question" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between Spanish and English modes?">
    Yes, you can toggle between language modes in your app settings. You can also create bilingual apps that detect user language preferences automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Spanish Mode support regional dialects?">
    Spanish Mode uses neutral Spanish by default, but you can customize your prompts to target specific regional variations like Mexican Spanish or European Spanish.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my existing prompts work in Spanish Mode?">
    Your existing English prompts will need to be translated or adapted for Spanish Mode. The AI Assisted Builder can help you optimize prompts for Spanish-language interactions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can users switch languages within the app?">
    Yes, you can configure language switching functionality within your app to allow users to toggle between Spanish and other supported languages.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## <Icon icon="circle-exclamation" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Important Considerations

Always test your Spanish Mode apps with native speakers before launching to production. Language quality and cultural appropriateness are crucial for user satisfaction and engagement.

Spanish Mode is designed to make your apps accessible to Spanish-speaking audiences, but you maintain full control over the content and tone of interactions. Combine Spanish Mode with thoughtful prompt engineering for the best results.
