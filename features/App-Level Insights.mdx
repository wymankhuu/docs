App-level insights give app builders a dashboard for the apps they own: total users, sessions, average conversation length, common topics, and engagement over time. The dashboard ships in a follow-up release after V2 launch.

<Info>
 **Status: in development**

 The dashboard is on the V2 roadmap and ships in a follow-up release. Until then, [workspace-level activity](https://learn.playlab.ai/getstarted/Reviewing%20Activity) gives per-class data and [org-level monitoring](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/Reviewing%20App%20Activity) gives admin-level visibility.

## What's planned

The dashboard surfaces, for each app you own:

- Total active users in the last 7, 30, and 90 days
- Total sessions and average session length
- Conversation topics and common patterns
- Activity over time, by user, by org if shared cross-org

The view is owner-scoped. You see the data for the apps you built. Workspace managers and org admins continue to use the workspace and org views for their scope.

## What you can do today

Until the app-level dashboard ships, two views give you most of the data:

**Workspace activity.** If your app is used in workspaces you have access to, the Activity view in each workspace shows sessions and conversations. See [Reviewing student activity per class](https://learn.playlab.ai/getstarted/Reviewing%20Activity).

**Org-level monitoring.** Org admins have a cross-workspace view that filters by app. See [Centralized org monitoring](https://learn.playlab.ai/features/Reviewing%20App%20Activity).

If you need data the existing views do not surface, email [support@playlab.ai](mailto:support@playlab.ai). We are tracking requests as we build the dashboard.

The exact date is set by the engineering rollout. Watch the [Q2 2026 changelog](https://learn.playlab.ai/changelog/Playlab%20Q2%202026%20%E2%80%94%20V2%20Launch) for updates.
