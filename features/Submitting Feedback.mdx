## <Icon icon="message-lines" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> What is this feature?

The Submit Your Feedback feature allows Playlab users to share their experiences, suggestions, bug reports, and feature requests directly with the Playlab team. Your input helps us continuously improve Playlab and build features that matter most to our community. 

Whether you've discovered a bug, have an idea for a new feature, or want to share how Playlab has helped you, we want to hear from you.

<Note>
  All feedback is reviewed by our team. While we may not be able to respond to every submission individually, your input directly influences our product roadmap.
</Note>

## <Icon icon="lightbulb" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Rationale for the feature

User feedback is essential for helping us build a Playlab that reflects our community. that truly serves our community's needs. By collecting insights from builders, educators, and creators using Playlab, we can identify pain points, discover new use cases, and prioritize features that will have the greatest impact. Your feedback helps us understand how Playlab fits into your workflow and where we can make meaningful improvements.

## <Icon icon="circle-info" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Tips for effective feedback

    <li>Be specific about the issue or suggestion you're sharing</li>
    <li>Include screenshots or screen recordings when reporting visual issues</li>
    <li>Describe the impact the issue has on your workflow</li>
    <li>For feature requests, explain the problem you're trying to solve</li>
    <li>Mention your use case and how you're using Playlab</li>

## <Icon icon="circle-question" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How quickly will I hear back about my feedback?">
    We aim to acknowledge all feedback within 48 hours. Complex issues or feature requests are not guaranteed to make it into the product but all requests will be looged. If we pick up the request, itmay take longer to fully address, but we'll keep you updated on progress.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I submit anonymous feedback?">
    Yes, you can choose to submit feedback anonymously. However, providing your contact information allows us to follow up for clarification if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What types of feedback are most helpful?">
    All feedback is valuable! Bug reports with clear reproduction steps, feature requests with specific use cases, and detailed user experience feedback are particularly helpful for our development process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you implement all suggested features?">
    While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully review all feedback and prioritize features based on user needs, technical feasibility, and alignment with our product vision.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I suggest improvements to existing features?">
    Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for improving existing functionality. Please describe what's working well and what could be enhanced.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how much feedback I can submit?">
    No limit! We encourage ongoing feedback as you continue using Playlab. Regular users often provide the most valuable insights into our platform.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do you prioritize feedback and feature requests?">
    We prioritize based on factors like user impact, frequency of requests, alignment with our roadmap, and technical complexity. Critical bugs always take priority.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
