Publishing makes an app available beyond the people you have shared it with directly. You publish from the app builder or from any app's Share modal, set the visibility level, and optionally share with specific people, groups, or organizations in the same flow.

## Visibility levels

**Private.** Only you, the people you have shared with directly, and your org's admins can see the app. The most restrictive level; appropriate for class-specific apps you do not want spreading.

**Organization.** Members of your organization can see the app. They do not need to be in your workspace. Useful when you want everyone in the org to be able to find and use the app.

**Public.** Anyone with a Playlab account can find and use the app. Public apps appear in the Playlab community gallery.

If you want a more granular target than these levels, use the Share modal to add specific people, groups, or organizations. They see the app even when the broader visibility is Private.

## Coming from V1?

V1 used "Workspace only / Playlab / Public" as visibility levels. V2 replaces "Workspace only" with direct sharing and an "Organization" level that doesn't require workspace membership. The publishing button and basic flow are the same.

For the side-by-side workflow comparison, see the [V1 → V2 cheat sheet](/whatsnew/v2/V1%20to%20V2%20Cheat%20Sheet).

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I unpublish my app?">
    Yes. From the app builder or Share modal, toggle Publish off. The app stops being visible to viewers and disappears from search. Sharing with specific people stays in place but those people see the unpublished state.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I update my app after publishing?">
    Yes. Edit the app in the builder and click Publish again. The new version replaces the previous one for everyone with access. Conversations from the previous version are preserved.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my published app?">
    It depends on the visibility level you picked. See [Visibility levels](#visibility-levels) above, or the deeper guide at [Publishing your app in V2](/features/Publishing).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
