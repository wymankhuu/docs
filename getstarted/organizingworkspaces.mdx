## <Icon icon="folders" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Organizing Your Workspaces

Effectively organize your public-facing workspace by subject area, grade level, or role to help users find the right apps quickly. It's important to keep your organization and the workspaces in it logically sorted in ways that meet your workflows and the workflows of your team.

<Tip>
We recommend having clear titles and descriptions in your apps and workspace names that help users navigate in your organization/workspaces/apps.
</Tip>

## <Icon icon="circle-info" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Implementation Tips

Remember that successful organization is an iterative process:
- Develop a consistent naming convention
- Sort and categorize apps based on user needs
- Regularly review and update your organization system
- Get feedback from users on discoverability

<Tip>
Maintaining a clean and well-organized workspace not only improves user experience but also increases adoption rates and overall satisfaction with your Playlab implementation.
</Tip>
