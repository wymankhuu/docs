## Big ideas about AI

These ideas shape how we build Playlab. V2 follows the same approach.

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="AI as a thinking partner" icon="lightbulb">
 Playlab apps work best when they support a learner's thinking. We design for productive struggle.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Context is what makes AI useful for education" icon="book">
 A general AI assistant becomes a tutor when it has the right context: the curriculum, the standards, the student's prior work, the goal of the lesson. Playlab gives educators a way to assemble that context.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Trust and safety belong in the core" icon="shield">
 Privacy, flagging, and admin oversight are part of the product surface. Centralized monitoring and granular building permissions live alongside the build tools.
 </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if my org admin hasn't set anything up yet?">
    You can still sign in and explore. Your view will be limited until your admin invites you to a workspace or sets your role. If you can't make progress, ask your admin to add you to a workspace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I see other people's apps?">
    If they have shared an app with you or with a workspace you belong to, the app appears in your dashboard. Public apps are findable through search.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I undo something I did by accident?">
    Most actions in V2 are reversible. Deleting an app or workspace is not. If you delete by accident, contact support@playlab.ai within a few days and we can usually recover it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
