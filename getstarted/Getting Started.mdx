## What's new in V2

V2 keeps the V1 building experience and changes how content is shared, deployed, and monitored.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
 <Card title="For builders" icon="hammer" href="/whatsnew/v2/V2 for Builders">
 Your apps are private until you share them. New Editor role for collaboration. Cross-org deployment without remixing.
 </Card>
 <Card title="For educators" icon="graduation-cap" href="/whatsnew/v2/V2 for Educators">
 Curate apps as well as create them. Manager visibility into all member activity. Granular building permissions.
 </Card>
 <Card title="For admins" icon="shield" href="/whatsnew/v2/V2 for Admins">
 Org-level data, insights, and centralized monitoring of flags. Deploy at scale without workarounds.
 </Card>
 <Card title="For networks or districts" icon="globe" href="/whatsnew/v2/V2 for Networks or Districts">
 Multi-org deployment. Activity segmentation. Collections for curated app sets that update everywhere.
 </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Big ideas about AI

These ideas shape how we build Playlab. V2 follows the same approach.

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="AI as a thinking partner" icon="lightbulb">
 Playlab apps work best when they support a learner's thinking. We design for productive struggle.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Context is what makes AI useful for education" icon="book">
 A general AI assistant becomes a tutor when it has the right context: the curriculum, the standards, the student's prior work, the goal of the lesson. Playlab gives educators a way to assemble that context.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Trust and safety belong in the core" icon="shield">
 Privacy, flagging, and admin oversight are part of the product surface. V2 adds centralized monitoring and finer building permissions.
 </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
