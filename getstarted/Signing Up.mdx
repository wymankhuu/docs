Getting started with Playlab takes a few minutes. This page walks through creating an account, joining your organization, and getting to your first workspace.

## Creating your Playlab account

Playlab access starts with an invitation. You either receive one through your school, district, or organization, or you sign up through a Playlab event or program.

<Steps>
 <Step title="Find your invitation">
 Most invitations come by email. Some partners share an invite link that lets multiple people sign up at once.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Click the link or visit playlab.ai">
 The link takes you to a sign-up page. From playlab.ai directly, you sign up with email or single sign-on if your org supports it.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Set up your account">
 Add your name and a password (or use SSO). Confirm your email if asked.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Land in your organization">
 Your invitation usually places you in an existing organization. Your dashboard shows the workspaces and apps available to you.
 </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to start once you're in

After signup, the [Getting started in V2](https://learn.playlab.ai/getstarted/Getting%20Started) page is the recommended next stop. It walks you through the four containers and points to workflows by role.

If you came in through a partner program, your Learning Partner may have a specific path for you. Check with them or with your org admin if you're unsure where to start.

## Onboarding programs

Playlab runs Intro to Playlab workshops and Office Hours regularly. These sessions help new users get hands-on with the platform.

Visit [Office Hours](https://learn.playlab.ai/learn%20with%20us/office%20hours) and [Onboarding](https://learn.playlab.ai/learn%20with%20us/onboarding) for current schedules.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="I don't have an invitation. How do I get one?">
 Reach out to a Playlab partner organization, attend a Playlab event, or contact [support@playlab.ai](mailto:support@playlab.ai) to ask about access.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Can I use Playlab without joining an organization?">
 Most Playlab features assume you are part of an org. Individual accounts are supported for community-facing features like trying public apps, but the full V2 experience (workspaces, sharing, building) requires org membership.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="My organization uses SSO. How does that work?">
 If your org has SSO set up, you sign in with your work or school account. The first time you sign in, Playlab creates your account and places you in your org. Subsequent sign-ins go through your identity provider.
 </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What information does Playlab collect during signup?">
    Name, email, organization, and role. We do not collect demographic information from teachers or students at signup. The full privacy policy is at playlab.ai/privacy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I sign up with a personal email if my org uses SSO?">
    Org admins decide. Some orgs require SSO for all members. Others allow personal emails. Ask your admin if you're unsure.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to activate an account?">
    Immediate for SSO. Email signups require clicking a confirmation link, which usually arrives within a few minutes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
