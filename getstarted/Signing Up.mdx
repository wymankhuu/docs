## <Icon icon="user-plus" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Creating Your Playlab Account

Getting started with Playlab is quick and easy. This guide will walk you through the process of creating your account and setting up your profile.

<Warning>
  **Playlab is currently invitation-only!**  
  
  To sign up for Playlab, you must:
  
  * Receive a workspace invitation link from an existing Playlab user or administrator
  * Click the unique invitation link sent to your email
  * Complete the sign-up process within 7 days of receiving your invitation
</Warning>

## <Icon icon="arrow-decision" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Request access through one of these options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Playlab 60 Minute Virtual Onboarding">
    **Join a 60 minute Virtual onboarding session hosted by a Playlab Lead Facilitator to get a complete walkthrough of Playlab features and receive your invitation.**
    
    <Steps className="space-y-2">
      <Step title="Sign up for an onboarding session">Sign up for a [60 Minute Playlab Onboarding Session](https://calendly.com/playlab-onboarding/playlab-onboarding)</Step>
      <Step title="Receive your invitation">Receive your invitation via email to join Playlab before the session</Step>
      <Step title="Create your account">Create your account using the invitation link sent to your email</Step>
      <Step title="Complete your profile">Complete your profile with relevant information</Step>
      <Step title="Join the session">Show up and learn in community with the Playlab team!</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Join the Relay Playlab PLC">
    **Join educators and non profit leaders using AI to create meaningful tools for themselves and others in the Relay GSE Playlab Professional Learning Community.**
    
    Along the way, you'll build your understanding of AI, explore opportunities and risks, build relationships with other educators, and create an actual AI tool to advance your work.
    
    <Steps className="space-y-2">
      <Step title="Sign up for the PLC">Sign up for the [Relay Playlab Professional Learning Community (PLC)](https://cvent.me/294zRn)</Step>
      <Step title="Select dates">Select the dates and times that work best for you</Step>
      <Step title="Receive your invitation">Receive your invitation after joining</Step>
      <Step title="Create your account">Create your account using the invitation link sent to your email</Step>
      <Step title="Complete your profile">Complete your profile with relevant information</Step>
      <Step title="Join your cohort">Join your Relay PLC cohort!</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Attend a Workshop or Event">
    **Participate in a Playlab workshop or event to learn about the platform and receive an invitation.**
    
    <Steps className="space-y-2">
      <Step title="Look for opportunities">Be on the lookout for Playlab facilitated workshops or partner led affiliated workshops</Step>
      <Step title="Register">Register for an upcoming Playlab workshop or event</Step>
      <Step title="Join Playlab">Join Playlab!</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
