## <Icon icon="user-plus" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Creating Your Playlab Account

Getting started with Playlab is quick and easy. This guide will walk you through the process of creating your account and setting up your profile.

<Warning>
  **Playlab is currently invitation-only!**  
  
  To sign up for Playlab, you must:
  
  * Receive a workspace invitation link from an existing Playlab user or administrator
  * Click the unique invitation link sent to your email
  * Complete the sign-up process within 7 days of receiving your invitation
</Warning>
