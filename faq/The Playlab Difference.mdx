## Why Playlab is fundamentally different

Most AI-in-education tools give you a menu of pre-built templates: a lesson planner, a quiz generator, a rubric writer. You pick one, type in a prompt, and hope the output is good enough. You don't know which AI model is running underneath. You don't control how it reasons about your curriculum. And you can't customize it for the specific needs of your school, students, or community.

Playlab is different. We're a **nonprofit** building **open AI infrastructure for education** where educators, leaders, and students **build their own AI tools**, choose their own models, and maintain full ownership and transparency over how AI works in their context. Here's what that looks like in practice:

## How Playlab Compares

|  | Other AI Tools | Playlab |
| :---- | ----- | ----- |
| **Approach** | ❌ Pre-built templates <br /> You consume what's offered (lesson planner, quiz maker, rubric writer) | ✅ Build your own <br /> You **build your own AI apps**, fully customized for your context, and **remix others' pre-built apps** to adapt them |
| **Model Transparency** | ❌ Hidden <br /> Platform picks the cheapest AI model behind the scenes; you never know what's running | ✅ You choose <br /> You **choose which AI model** powers each tool and see exactly what you're using |
| **Knowledge Quality** | ❌ Basic search <br /> Upload a document; AI keyword-searches it and hopes for the best | ✅ Knowledge graphing <br /> **Standards are linked relationally** so the AI understands how concepts connect, not just where words appear |
| **Student Agency** | ❌ Passive use <br /> Students interact with pre-built tools | ✅ Students build <br /> Students **build their own AI apps**, developing real AI literacy and computational fluency |
| **Safety & Visibility** | ❌ Limited <br /> Limited moderation and visibility into student interactions | ✅ Full visibility <br /> **Full conversation visibility for teachers**, automated safety flags, customizable guardrails, and admin data exports |
| **Mission** | ❌ For-profit <br /> Companies focused on maximizing user growth and seat count | ✅ Nonprofit <br /> 501(c)(3) focused on impact with mission-aligned partnerships, not sales quotas |
| **Ownership** | ❌ Closed <br /> Your data lives in someone else's product; you can't see or control the internals | ✅ Open infrastructure <br /> You control what you build, how it works, and who it serves |
| **Services** | ❌ Software only <br /> Software licenses with optional PD add-ons | ✅ Full support <br /> Workshops, PLCs, Design Fellowships, custom app development, and change management support |
