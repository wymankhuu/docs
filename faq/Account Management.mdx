## How do I get access to Playlab?

You get access by joining an [onboarding workshop](https://calendly.com/playlab-onboarding/playlab-onboarding), the [Relay Playlab PLC](https://cvent.me/294zRn), or by being invited by a Playlab partner.

## Can I invite other folks to join the onboarding?

Of course! Please share the onboarding registration link with anybody you think might benefit from learning about Playlab.

## Can I share my account with others?

No, you may not make your access credentials or account available to others outside your organization. You are responsible for all activities that occur using your credentials.

## What happens if I violate the Terms?

Playlab may suspend or terminate your access to the Services without notice for material breaches of the Term of Service.

## How does Playlab handle copyright complaints?

If you believe your intellectual property rights have been infringed, send notice to [support@playlab.ai](mailto:support@playlab.ai) with the required information. Playlab may delete or disable content alleged to be infringing and terminate accounts of repeat infringers.
