## <Icon icon="play" size="24" className="inline-block align-text-bottom" /> Learn Through Play and Testing

Prompting with AI is a learned skill that requires play and tinkering. As AI continues to evolve, how we interact with and build AI will also evolve as well. We encourage all builders to learn through building and think that Playlab is a great place to do so!

<Note>
Effective prompting is both an art and a science. Keep experimenting, learning from results, and refining your approach.
</Note>
