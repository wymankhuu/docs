### <Icon icon="newspaper" size="20" className="inline-block align-text-bottom mr-2" /> Denver Students Prepare Community for Elections

**Youth-Led Civic Engagement through AI**

<a href="https://www.npr.org/2024/02/15/denver-students-ai-voting" target="_blank">
  <img 
    src="/images/denver-students.jpg"

NPR highlighted how students in Denver used Playlab to create AI applications that helped prepare their community for the 2024 elections. These student-designed tools provided accessible information about ballot measures, voting locations, and candidate positions in multiple languages.

**Built by**: Denver South High School AP Government Students
**Playlab Apps**: [Denver Votes 2024](https://app.playlab.ai/denver-votes)

**Impact**: The student-created apps were accessed by over 5,000 community members and credited with increasing youth voter registration by 23% in participating neighborhoods.
